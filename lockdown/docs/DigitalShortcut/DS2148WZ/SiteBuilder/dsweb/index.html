Who are we?

Digital Shortcut Inc. mission is to deliver low cost, but versatile and powerful, easy-to-use design and development platforms for embedded and network oriented applications (embedded ethernet). We aim to reduce startup effort and time to market; from the concept through a prototype to final product. Our expertise in microprocessors, FPGAs and the manufacturing process ensures that the difficult task of designing embedded project from scratch or redesigning the old stuff is not "so risky" anymore.

Our favorite "Shortcuts"

Shortcut #1: DS2148WZ (ARM7 with Fast Network Processing Unit)

NXP LPC2148 and WIZnet W5300 Combo.
Feel the power of a duo ARM7 and WIZnet W5300 (hardware TCP/IP stack). Our implementation of http server serves the page you are reading now in 60ms and in the same time doesn't load the CPU that much. All peripherals and interrupts of LPC2148 are ready for your application.

Shortcut #2: DSX50WZ (Spartan-3 FPGA with Fast Network Processing Unit)

Xilinx Spartan-3(XC3S50AN) and WIZnet W5300 Combo.
Amazing! The smallest Spartan-3 capable of serving this web page in less than 50ms from internal flash memory. Our demo http server runs on PicoBlaze leaving 2/3 of Spartan resources available to you. No need for heavy IP modules, DDR's, OS and 8-layer PCB.
Connect your VHDL/Verilog code to the net instantly!

Shortcut #3: DS2148BB (NXP LPC2148 ARM7 Development Board)

LPC2148 Bare Bone QuickStart Board
Our workhorse. LPC2148 with USB and RS232 ports. It is ready for you when you need a fast, single chip processor with ample of Flash, RAM, good selection of sophisticated peripherals and USB connectivity. We prototyped most of our contracting jobs using this board. If your background is 8051 (or any other 8biters) reclaim the fun of programming embedded systems.
