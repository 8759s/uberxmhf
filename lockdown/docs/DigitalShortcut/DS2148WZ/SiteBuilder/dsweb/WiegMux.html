Wiegand Reader Multiplexer

Reliable * Small * Portable * Inexpensive   $99    

Digital Shortcut Inc. Wiegand Reader Expander is an easy way to add extra reader to the system without need for additional cables to the access controller and without using next port. It multiplexes (combines) signals from two readers. In case of simulaneus scans it buffers data from one reader and sends it seconds later. In the case when all available ports are already taken adding a new reader can be quite costly. It is a perfect solution when another reader is needed in close proximity to an existing one. Typical applications: adding second reader to busy lobby door entrance or elevated reader for trucks when passenger car reader is already in service.
